The Origin Story

How a Tiny Team in Hyderabad Changed the Game

It started with a frustration. In 2023, co-founders Yashrab Unnisa and Priya Nair were working at a large IT firm and watching brilliant product ideas die slow deaths at the hands of bloated processes, bureaucratic agencies and sky-high retainers that only the biggest companies could afford.

They quit on the same day. They built their first client's MVP in 6 weeks from a rented desk in Hyderabad. The client — a fintech startup — went on to raise ₹40 crore in Series A. Word spread fast.

By 2024, TechEchos had 20 engineers and a reputation for shipping faster than anyone thought possible without sacrificing quality. By 2025, we'd expanded into AI and cloud services. By 2026, we crossed 200 shipped products and opened our first international office.

Today, TechEchos is the engineering partner that ambitious founders call first — not because we're the cheapest or the flashiest, but because we genuinely care about what we build and for whom we build it.
