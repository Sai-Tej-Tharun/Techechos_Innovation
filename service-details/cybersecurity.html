Service Overview

The question is no longer whether your product will be attacked — it's when, and whether you'll be ready. Enterprise clients have security teams that will grill your team with detailed questionnaires. Consumer products handle personal data governed by GDPR. Healthcare applications must meet HIPAA. Fintech products need SOC 2. And every public-facing API is under constant automated attack.

Our security practice is built on the philosophy that security is a property of your architecture, not a product you purchase. We integrate security thinking into every phase of development — from threat modelling during architecture design to dependency scanning in CI/CD to penetration testing before major releases.

We have helped startups pass enterprise security reviews that would have otherwise killed six-figure deals. We have helped scale-ups achieve SOC 2 Type II in 12 months. We have identified critical vulnerabilities in production systems before attackers did. Security is not our afterthought — it is our standard.

Core Capabilities

Penetration Testing

Web app, API, mobile and infrastructure penetration testing following OWASP methodology. Detailed report with CVSS scores, exploitation proof and step-by-step remediation.

Security Architecture Review

Threat modelling, architecture review and security design consultation — the earlier in your build cycle, the cheaper the fixes.

Compliance Programmes

SOC 2 Type I & II, ISO 27001, GDPR and HIPAA compliance programmes. Policy documentation, evidence collection, control implementation and audit preparation.

SAST / DAST in CI/CD

Static application security testing, dynamic testing and dependency vulnerability scanning integrated into your CI/CD pipeline — security gates on every pull request.

Security Monitoring

SIEM integration, anomaly detection, threat intelligence feeds and incident response playbooks — 24/7 visibility into your security posture.

Security Training

Developer security awareness training, secure coding workshops and social engineering simulations — reducing human-layer risk across your team.

How We Deliver
01

Threat Modelling

STRIDE analysis of your architecture to identify attack surfaces, trust boundaries and high-risk components before testing begins.

02

Reconnaissance & Scanning

OSINT, subdomain enumeration, port scanning and automated vulnerability identification across all in-scope assets.

03

Manual Penetration Testing

Manual exploitation of identified vulnerabilities by our security engineers — OWASP Top 10, BOLA, injection, auth bypass and business logic flaws.

04

Findings Report

CVSS-scored vulnerability report with exploitation evidence, risk rating, business impact and step-by-step remediation guidance prioritised by severity.

05

Remediation & Re-test

We work with your engineering team to remediate findings and then re-test every vulnerability to confirm it is closed before issuing a clean certificate.
