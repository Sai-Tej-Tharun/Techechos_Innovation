Service Overview

The AI revolution is real — but the gap between a compelling AI demo and a reliable production feature is larger than most teams anticipate. We've shipped 15+ LLM-powered features and trained custom models across computer vision, NLP and time-series prediction. We know the problems that don't show up in notebooks: hallucination rates, latency, cost at scale, evaluation frameworks, and the careful prompt engineering required for consistent behaviour.

Our AI practice covers the full spectrum: from integrating OpenAI's APIs into an existing product in a single sprint, to training and deploying custom YOLO-based computer vision models that run in real-time on edge hardware. We are opinionated about tooling — we use Python and the PyTorch ecosystem for custom models, and LangChain or plain OpenAI SDKs for LLM features depending on the complexity of the orchestration required.

Critically, we build evaluation frameworks before we build features. You cannot ship an AI feature responsibly without a way to measure whether it's working. Our eval pipeline is the foundation of every AI engagement.
